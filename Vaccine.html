🐾 Vaccination Tips

  1. 1. Never miss booster doses.
  2. 2. Observe pets for 24 hours after vaccination.
  3. 3. Do not vaccinate a sick or weak pet.
  4. 4. Avoid bathing immediately after vaccination.
  5. 5. Check vaccine expiry date.
  6. 6. Keep emergency vet contact saved.
  7. 7. Inform vet about previous allergies.
  8. 8. Provide fresh water after vaccination.
  9. 9. Monitor appetite and activity levels.
  10. 10. Consult your vet if symptoms persist.
