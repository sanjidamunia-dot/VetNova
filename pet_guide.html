PET CARE GUIDE
🐾 A happy pet is a healthy pet!
Simple tips and essential care to keep your pet happy, healthy and active.

REMEMBER

  • ✔ Love them
  • ✔ Care for them
  • ✔ Be patient
  • ✔ Keep them safe
    • 1

    Healthy Food 🍖

    Give nutritious food suitable for your pet's age, size and breed. Always provide fresh water..

    2

    Clean Environment🏠

    Keep your pet's living area clean and dry. Regularly clean bowls, bedding and toys.

    3

    Exercise & Play 🎾

    Daily exercise and play keep your pet active, happy and healthy. It also strengthens your bond.

    4

    Grooming 🪮

    Brush your pet regularly. Keep their coat, ears, eyes and nails clean to prevent health problems.

    5

    Health Care ❤️

    Regular vet check-ups, vaccinations and de-worming keep your pet protected from diseases.

    6

    Training🐕

    Train your pet with positive reinforcement. Be patient and consistent.

    7

    Love & Attention 💗

    Spend quality time every day. Your love and attention make your pet feel safe and happy.

    8

    Safety First 🛡️

    Keep harmful items out of reach. Use a leash outside and ensure proper ID tags.

    🐶 Every pet deserves love
    🏠 Safe & happy home
    ❤️ Care builds strong bond
